Why Markas Walet

Built on Three Core Values

Everything we do is guided by education, social impact, and traceability — the pillars of a sustainable swiftlet nest ecosystem.

Edu-Agri Tech

Through proper education and appropriate technology, swiftlet farmers will optimize the harvest of swiftlet nest — driving higher yield and better livelihoods.

Social Impact

Our mission is to create jobs and better opportunities for swiftlet nest farmers across Indonesia, while pioneering foreign exchange for our country.

Traceability

The market price of swiftlet nests is significantly higher when the swiftlet house is traceable. We build trust with technology-backed quality verification.
